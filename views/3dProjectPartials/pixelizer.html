Pixelizer (Autodesk Maya, Python)
Source Code
This was a mini project I worked on to start learning scripting in Maya. It was just after the "Pixels" video by Patrick Jean came out on YouTube. Basically what it does is utilize a selected 3d model and "voxelize" it. I realize now I should've called the script Voxelizer. It'll find the closest whole number coordinate and place a cube there.
