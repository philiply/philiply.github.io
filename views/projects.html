Three.js Sampler (JavaScript, WebGL)
Took the time to look into Three.js. It's a great (and easy) way to get into WebGL if you don't want to deal with the lower level intricacies. Here is a scene with a reflective sphere and a helix of spheres around it. I also had the chance to look into mouse controls for it, so you can click and drag (or touch!) the scene. You can even zoom in and out.
